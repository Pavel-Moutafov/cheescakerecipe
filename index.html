Please Make This Cheesecake

Recipe by: Doug

I've been making this cheesecake since I was little, growing up in San Francisco. This recipe has been passed down through generations of my family, from my grandma to my mom and now to me. It is by far the best and the easiest recipe on the internet. Today I will show you how to make it.

Ingredients

  • Measuring Spoons
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 200g Cream Cheese
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups softened butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • Graham Crackers
  • Nuclear Bomb
  1. Preheat your oven to 325ยบ
  2. Combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and brown sugar
  3. Add in melted butter and stir well
  4. Put crumbs into 9" pan and press hard
  5. Press harder
  6. In a stand mixer, stir the cream cheese until stirred. Not more or less
  7. Add in vanilla extract and salt until added in. Scrape sides of the bowl so that they are scraped well
  8. Put the mixer on low speed and add in one egg at a time, incorporate them until they are incorporated
  9. Put the batter onto the cookie bottom and bake until they are baked
  10. Remove from heat and let cool until cooled
  11. Add orange zest and nuclear bomb to taste
  12. Enjoy!
